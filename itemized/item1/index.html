Dog Days of Summer

August 7 - 8, 2020

Organized Shoot

The Dog Days of Summer Shootout is a 150 Target International Trap Match/ PA Junior Olympic Championships.

  • Lunch is included Saturday and Sunday

  • Cost $125.00 Cash or Check.

  • PA Junior Olympics Championships. (USA Shooting Sectioned Event)

  • The first 125 Targets will be used to determine the PA USA Shooting Junior Olympic Championships. Out of state shooters are encouraged to attend. They will not be eligible for State Medals but Out of State Awards will be given. The Shooter must be a Member of USA Shooting to shoot for qualification prior to the event.

  • Practice on Friday 8/8/2020 from 10:00 till 6:00

Course of fire:

  • Saturday 8/9/2020: 75 Target on Saturday with Junior Final
  • Sunday 8/9/2020: 75 Targets on Sunday with Open Final

Bring the Family, our range is 12 minutes from Hershey Amusement Park:

  • Palmyra Sportsmens Association
  • 410 Sportmen Road Annville PA 17003

Categories

  • Open- Women- Senior - Junior Classes
  • A-B-C-D\

Course of fire:

Friday, August 7th, 2020

  • 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Open Practice: Rounds are $5.00 Cash or Check

Saturday, August 8th, 2020

  • 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Day 1: 3 Rounds (75 Targets) + Junior Final (50 Targets) ISSF/ USA Shooting Format

Sunday, August 9th 28, 2020

  • 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Day 2: 3 Rounds (75 Targets) + Open Final (50 Targets) ISSF/ USA Shooting Format
  • Juniors are able to shoot in both finals if they shoot the scores.

To register email J.Thompson@palmyrasportsmens.com
Any questions please call or Text 717-329-2314
